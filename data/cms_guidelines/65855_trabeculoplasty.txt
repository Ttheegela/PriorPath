PROCEDURE: Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT / ALT)
CPT CODE: 65855
SOURCE: CMS LCD / Payer Clinical Policy

COVERED INDICATIONS

Laser trabeculoplasty (Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty [SLT] or Argon Laser Trabeculoplasty [ALT], CPT 65855)
is medically necessary when ALL of the following are met:

1. Confirmed Diagnosis
   - Primary open-angle glaucoma (POAG), ICD-10 H40.11x
   - Pseudoexfoliative glaucoma, ICD-10 H40.14x
   - Pigmentary glaucoma, ICD-10 H40.13x
   - Ocular hypertension (OHT), ICD-10 H40.05x — when IOP consistently > 24 mmHg on two readings

2. Inadequate IOP Control (any of the following)
   - Target IOP not achieved on maximum tolerated medical therapy (one or more topical agents)
   - Patient intolerant of or non-compliant with topical glaucoma medications
   - As primary therapy (SLT) when medication side effects, cost, or adherence is a concern

3. Open Angle Confirmed
   - Gonioscopy documenting Shaffer grade ≥ 2 (open, non-appositional angle) in all quadrants
   - Closed-angle glaucoma is NOT a covered indication for trabeculoplasty

4. Documentation of Glaucomatous Damage or Risk
   - Optic nerve evaluation: vertical cup-to-disc ratio ≥ 0.6 or asymmetry ≥ 0.2 between eyes, OR
   - Documented glaucomatous visual field defect (Humphrey 24-2 or 30-2) consistent with optic nerve findings

REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION

- IOP measurements on current therapy (two readings, different visits)
- Gonioscopy confirming open angle
- Optic nerve photograph (stereoscopic disc photos or OCT RNFL)
- Visual field test (Humphrey 24-2 or equivalent) within 6 months
- List of current and prior glaucoma medications with documented reason for inadequacy or intolerance
- Clinical notes confirming diagnosis of POAG, PXF glaucoma, pigmentary glaucoma, or OHT

REPEAT TREATMENT

- Repeat SLT (same eye) covered when prior response was ≥ 20% IOP reduction that subsequently waned
- Minimum 12 months between treatments in the same eye
- Documentation of prior treatment response required

COVERAGE LIMITATIONS

- Not covered for acute angle-closure glaucoma (different procedure: iridotomy, CPT 66761)
- Not covered as a purely cosmetic or elective procedure
- ALT and SLT are interchangeable for coverage purposes; SLT preferred per current guidelines
